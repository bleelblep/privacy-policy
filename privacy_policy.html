Privacy Policy

Pixlings

How we handle your data — spoiler: we don't.

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

The short version: Pixlings operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. The app has no internet permission.

Overview

Pixlings is developed by Bleelblep. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Android application designed for Nothing Phone devices.

Information We Access

To provide its functionality, Pixlings accesses certain device information locally:

Device Sensors and Status

  • Battery Status: We monitor your device's charging state and battery level to display relevant animations on the Glyph interface.
  • Motion Sensors: We access accelerometer data to detect device movement and trigger motion-based animations. This data is processed in real-time and is not stored.

Notifications

  • Pixlings uses Android's Notification Listener Service to detect when notifications arrive from apps you've selected.
  • We only access the package name (which app sent the notification) to trigger animations.
  • We do NOT read notification content, titles, messages, or any other notification details.

Phone State

  • We detect incoming calls to automatically pause animations, allowing system call UI to take priority.
  • We do NOT access call logs, phone numbers, or contact information.
  • This feature is optional and can be disabled in settings.

Installed Applications

  • We query your list of installed applications to let you choose which apps should trigger notification and music animations.
  • This information is used solely for the app selection interface and is stored locally on your device.

Media Playback

  • We detect which app is currently playing media to synchronize music animations.
  • We only access the package name of the media app, not the content being played.

Information Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Preferences: Your app settings, theme choices, and configuration options are stored in local SharedPreferences.
  • Database: Pixling configurations and your app selection preferences are stored in a local SQLite database.
  • Custom Animations: Any custom pixling animations you import are stored in the app's private storage directory.

None of this data is transmitted to external servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Personal identification Location data Contacts Calendar Camera or microphone SMS messages Call logs or history Browsing history Device identifiers Analytics or usage stats Notification content Network activity

Third-Party Services

Pixlings does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not contain ads and has no internet access.

The app uses the Nothing Glyph SDK solely to control the Glyph LED interface on compatible Nothing Phone devices. This SDK operates locally and does not transmit data externally.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device encryption and app sandboxing.

Children's Privacy

Pixlings does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal data from any users, children can safely use the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
